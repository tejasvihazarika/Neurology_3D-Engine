    Signal Processing Steps

    How we turned raw brain signals into useful information, step by step.

    Step 1: Breaking Down the Signal (FFT)

    FFT splits the brain signal into different speed patterns (frequencies). Think of it like separating music into bass, mid, and treble.

    Step 2: Brain Wave Types

    Brain waves are grouped by speed: Delta (slow/sleep), Theta (drowsy), Alpha (relaxed), Beta (focused), Gamma (thinking hard).

    Step 3: Which Brain Area?

    We map signals to brain regions: Frontal (thinking), Parietal (sensing), Temporal (hearing/memory), Occipital (seeing).
